The GOAT Sandwich

This is the pinnacle of all sandwiches. A simple yet brilliant combination.

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 slices of your favourite bread (get wholemeal and be healthy mate)
  • Peanut butter (crunchy if you're daring)
  • Cut up dill pickles (the more the merrier

INSTRUCTIONS

I mean...do you really need instructions?? Here it is incase this is your first sandwich rodeo:
  1. Spread peanut butter on both slices of your bread. (Toasting is optional)
  2. Place pickles in a pattern of your choice on one slice
  3. Cover with the other slice
  4. Salivate at the sheer beauty of your creation and take a monsterous bite
